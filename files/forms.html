Accepted to WUNS? Complete the steps below to finalize your child’s enrollment.

If your child has been accepted and you would like to accept the offer of admission, complete the required forms below to confirm your child’s place.

Forms Required After Acceptance


Complete the following forms based on your enrollment status:

  • Enrollment Form: Required every year your child attends WUNS. Submit this form now to confirm enrollment.
  • Application Form: Only complete this form if you are applying for admission (not needed if your child has already been accepted).
  • Developmental History Form: Required once after your child has been accepted into the school.
  • Medical Forms (PDFs): Download, complete, and return both required medical forms listed below.
