Forms

Accepted to WUNS? Complete the steps below to finalize your child’s enrollment.

If your child has been accepted and you would like to accept the offer of admission, please complete the required forms below to confirm your child’s place at the nursery school.

Forms Required After Acceptance

  • Enrollment Form: This form is required every year your child attends WUNS. It confirms your child’s enrollment for the upcoming school year.
  • Developmental History Form: This form is required once after your child has been accepted. It provides important background information to the school.
  • Application for Admission Form: This form is for new applicants only and should not be completed if your child has already been accepted.
  • Medical Forms (PDFs): These forms must be downloaded, completed, and returned to the nursery school.
