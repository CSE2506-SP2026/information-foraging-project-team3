Application Process

A step-by-step guide from initial inquiry to completed enrollment.

Before You Apply

Schedule a Visit

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should contact the school to schedule a visit.

Step 1: Submit an Application

An application fee of $40 is required when applying to the nursery school. After the fee is received, your child is placed on an enrollment list.

You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date.

Application for Admission

Complete the Application for Admission online: Application for Admission Form

You may also print and return the PDF version: Application for Admission WUNS (PDF)

Deposit Upon Acceptance

Upon acceptance, a nonrefundable deposit of one month’s tuition is due. This deposit will be applied to tuition for the following May.

Step 2: After Acceptance

Families who accept an offer of admission must complete the required forms below to confirm enrollment.

Required Forms

Enrollment Form (Required Every Year)

This form confirms your child’s enrollment for the upcoming school year and must be completed annually.

Developmental History Form (Required Once After Acceptance)

This form provides important background information about your child.

Required Medical Forms (Download, Complete, and Return)

  • Child Medical Examination Report (PDF)
  • Immunization Consent/History (PDF)
